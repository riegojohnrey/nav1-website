Navigational aids

Navigational aid (NAVAID), also known as aid to navigation (ATON), is any sort of signal, markers or quidance equipment which aids the traveler in navigation, usually nautical or aviation travel.

Examples of Navigational aids

  • Gyro Compass
  • Radar
  • Magnetic Compass
  • Navigational Lights
  • Ship Whistle
  • Voyage Plan
  • GPS information
